SharePath Carsharing Rules

Here are the main points of the rules:
1) Park only in designated areas. Do not park behind barriers or gates as this will block other drivers' access. Use the free parking spaces.
2) Remain within the designated zones. Do not venture outside them. Intercity journeys are permitted with pre-arranged stops.
3) In case of a accidental/unintentional vehicle breakdown, contact us for assistance. Send us photographs/videos and our team will dispatch a technician to resolve the issue. All damages will be covered, so do not worry about additional costs.
4) Any state fines incurred during or after the end of the lease term (before the next lease) will be automatically deducted!
5) In the event of an emergency/road accident, simply click "Report Accident" to receive assistance. We will respond and send an employee to you as soon as possible!
That is all! If you adhere to these regulations, you will have a pleasant experience with SharePath!

Additional charges may apply for:
- Trash and smoking: $50
- Animals (on floor, seats, or in hands): $50
- Oversized items (vans for that): $50
- Incorrect parking (no parking, behind fences, barriers, paid parking): $100
- Towing of a car: $50
- In case of intentional damage to the vehicle, you will be charged for the full amount of the damage.
- Transfer of vehicle control to a third party: $50
- Use of the wrong fuel (not the specified brand in the instructions): $200
- In case of failure to report an road accident (the exception is the inability to do so): total cost of the damage + $200
